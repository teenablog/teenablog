<h1>Functional Hydrogel Coating Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Functional hydrogel coatings are advanced polymer-based materials designed to enhance surface properties by providing responsiveness to environmental stimuli such as temperature, pH, and electric fields. These coatings are widely used for their exceptional lubrication, anti-fouling, and drug delivery capabilities. With their superior adhesion, durability, and impact resistance, functional hydrogel coatings find applications in medical devices, flexible electronics, and smart materials, among others.</p><p>
