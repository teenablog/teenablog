<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polyoxymethylene (POM) Resin, commonly known as acetal or polyacetal resin, is a high-performance engineering thermoplastic characterized by exceptional mechanical properties, thermal stability, and chemical resistance. These properties make POM resins ideal for applications requiring low friction, high stiffness, and dimensional stability. POM is widely used in various industries, including automotive, consumer electronics, industrial machinery, and medical devices, replacing metals and other traditional plastics in wear-critical applications.</p><p>
