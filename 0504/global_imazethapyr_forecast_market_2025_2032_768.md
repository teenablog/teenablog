<h1>Imazethapyr Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Imazethapyr is an organic heterocyclic herbicide that belongs to the imidazolinone compound group. It is also known as pusaite, imidazoethylnicotinic acid, and benzathazole. Imazethapyr has excellent herbicidal activity against various types of weeds, including sedge weeds, monocotyledonous weeds, broad-leaved weeds, and others. It works by inhibiting the biosynthesis of plant side-chain amino acids, ultimately leading to the destruction of proteins in weeds and their subsequent death.</p><p>
