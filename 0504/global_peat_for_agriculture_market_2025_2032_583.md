<h1>Peat for Agriculture Market, Global Outlook &amp; Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Peat for agriculture refers to the decomposed organic material derived from plant matter, typically found in wetlands. It is widely used in horticulture, agriculture, and soil improvement due to its excellent water retention and aeration properties. Peat is a key component in garden soil mixes, organic fertilizers, and plant substrates, aiding plant growth and increasing soil fertility. With its applications spanning across commercial farming, greenhouses, and gardening, peat remains a critical input in modern agricultural practices.</p><p>
