<h1>Polyethylene Pellets Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Polyethylene pellets are small, cylindrical or disc-shaped granules of polyethylene, typically ranging from a few millimeters in diameter. These pellets serve as a crucial raw material in the production of various plastic products. The pellets are transported to manufacturing sites, where they are melted down and molded into a variety of products for industrial, commercial, and consumer use. They are primarily used in producing packaging materials, bottles, films, toys, containers, and other molded plastic items.</p><p>
</p><p>Polyethylene itself is one of the most widely used polymers in the world, given its versatility, affordability, and durability. Polyethylene pellets are categorized based on their density and molecular structure into three main types: HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene), LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene), and LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene).
