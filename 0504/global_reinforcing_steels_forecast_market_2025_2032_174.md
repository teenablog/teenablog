<h1>Reinforcing Steels Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Reinforcing steels, commonly known as rebar, are an essential component in construction, particularly in reinforced concrete and masonry structures. Rebar is a steel bar or mesh of steel wires used to strengthen and support concrete, which is naturally strong under compression but weak when subjected to tensile forces. The key function of rebar is to significantly improve the tensile strength of concrete by acting as a tension device, thereby helping concrete to withstand bending, stretching, and other forces that would otherwise cause it to crack.</p><p>
</p><p>Rebar typically features a surface that is deformed (ribbed or textured) to ensure a better bond with the surrounding concrete, which enhances its ability to resist stress.
