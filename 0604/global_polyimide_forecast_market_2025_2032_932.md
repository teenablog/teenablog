<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polyimide (PI) is a class of high-performance polymers characterized by the presence of imide groups in their molecular structure. These polymers exhibit exceptional thermal stability, mechanical strength, chemical resistance, and electrical insulation properties, making them indispensable in various industries. PI is widely utilized in high-temperature environments, such as aerospace, electronics, automotive, and medical applications. Due to its durability and resilience, polyimide has become a preferred material for flexible printed circuits, insulation films, coatings, and advanced composites.</p><p>
