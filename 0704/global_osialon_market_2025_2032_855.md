They are often compared to alumina-based materials for their superior performance in these domains. The primary appeal of O-Sialon ceramics lies in their ability to withstand harsh environments, making them essential in applications where materials are exposed to high heat and aggressive substances. They are widely used in various industrial sectors, particularly where their unique properties are critical, such as in the handling of molten metals and in the chemical industry.</p><p>
</p><p>O-Sialon materials are primarily used in industries requiring high durability and corrosion resistance, especially in situations involving molten non-ferrous metals.
