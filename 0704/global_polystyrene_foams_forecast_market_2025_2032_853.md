<h1>Polystyrene Foams Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Polystyrene foams are lightweight, durable materials widely used across various industries for their excellent insulating and cushioning properties. These foams come in two primary forms: Expandable Polystyrene (EPS) and Extruded Polystyrene (XPS). EPS is commonly used in packaging and construction, whereas XPS is preferred for thermal insulation and moisture resistance. Polystyrene foams are crucial in protecting consumer goods, ensuring food safety, and enhancing energy efficiency in buildings.</p><p>
