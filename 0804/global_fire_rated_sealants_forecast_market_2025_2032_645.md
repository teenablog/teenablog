</p><p><strong>Fire Rated Sealants</strong> are specialized sealants designed to prevent the spread of fire, smoke, and toxic gases through openings in fire-rated assemblies such as walls, floors, and ceilings. These sealants play a critical role in building safety by maintaining the integrity of fire barriers and enhancing fire resistance.</p><p>
</p><p>They are commonly used in commercial buildings, residential complexes, industrial facilities, and infrastructure projects. Fire-rated sealants come in various types, including intumescent sealants, which expand when exposed to heat, and elastomeric sealants, which maintain flexibility while offering fire resistance.</p><p>
