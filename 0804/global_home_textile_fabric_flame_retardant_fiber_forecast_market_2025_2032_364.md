Restraints</p><p>

</p><p>2. Environmental Concerns: Some flame-retardant chemicals used in fiber manufacturing raise environmental and health concerns, leading to regulatory restrictions and consumer reluctance.</p><p>
</p><p>3. Performance Limitations: Certain flame-retardant treatments may compromise the softness, breathability, or color fastness of textiles, impacting their appeal to consumers.</p><p>
