</p><p>Vitreous Carbon, also known as glassy carbon, is a non-graphitized carbon material that combines glassy and ceramic properties with those of graphite. It is characterized by high-temperature resistance, exceptional resistance to chemical attack, and impermeability to gases and liquids. It finds significant applications as an electrode material in electrochemistry and for high-temperature crucibles.</p><p>
