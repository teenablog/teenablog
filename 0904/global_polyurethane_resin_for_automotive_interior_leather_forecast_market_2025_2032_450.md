<strong>Overview</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polyurethane (PU) resin is a crucial material used in the production of synthetic leather for automotive interiors. This resin is favored for its durability, flexibility, and eco-friendly properties. Unlike traditional solvent-based alternatives, modern PU resins for automotive interior leather are primarily solvent-free, significantly reducing volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions. These environmentally friendly synthetic leathers are extensively utilized in middle and high-end automobiles, offering a balance of aesthetics, comfort, and sustainability.</p><p>
