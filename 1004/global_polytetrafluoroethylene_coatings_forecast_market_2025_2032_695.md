<h1>Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) Coatings Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Polytetrafluoroethylene (PTFE) coatings are high-performance, non-stick fluoropolymer coatings that utilize a two-coat system comprising a primer and a topcoat. PTFE coatings are renowned for their exceptional resistance to high temperatures, chemical corrosion, and abrasion, while also exhibiting one of the lowest coefficients of friction among solid materials. These coatings are widely used in industries such as chemical processing, oil and gas, electrical and electronics, construction, and medical applications due to their durability and functional advantages.</p><p>
