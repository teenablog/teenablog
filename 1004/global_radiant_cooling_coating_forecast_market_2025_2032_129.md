<h1>Radiant Cooling Coating Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>






</p><p>Radiant cooling coatings are advanced materials designed to enhance heat dissipation from surfaces by increasing thermal conductivity and promoting heat radiation. Composed of components such as resin, thermal conductive powder, graphene, carbon nanotubes, and heat dissipation powder, these coatings can be electrostatically sprayed onto radiators of various shapes. They offer properties like wear resistance, chemical stability, and high-temperature resistance, making them suitable for applications in automobiles, architecture, industry, and more.</p><p>
