</p><p>Electrical insulation crepe paper is a specialized insulating material used in electrical applications. It is manufactured through a creping process, which enhances its flexibility, elasticity, and surface area. This type of insulation paper is primarily utilized in transformers, cables, and other electrical equipment to prevent electrical leakage and improve operational efficiency. The crepe paper's unique texture allows it to conform to irregular shapes, making it an ideal choice for high-voltage insulation applications.</p><p>
