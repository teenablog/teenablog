A rebar coupler connects two sections of reinforcing bars (rebars) that are used to reinforce concrete structures such as high-rise buildings, bridges, and nuclear plants. This system ensures the connection between rebars, offering an efficient and durable solution for reinforcing concrete while also minimizing the length of the reinforcement bars required.</p><p>
