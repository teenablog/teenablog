Developed by Imperial Chemical Industries (ICI) in 1978, PEEK has become a preferred material in industries requiring durability and superior performance. Its excellent electrical properties and processing capabilities make it indispensable in demanding applications, including aerospace, automotive, electronics, healthcare, and energy.</p><p>
