<strong>Market Size</strong></p><p>
</p><p>Polyethylene Terephthalate (PET), also referred to as PETE, is a type of engineering resin primarily used in packaging, textiles, and other industrial applications. It is a thermoplastic polymer resin of the polyester family, widely utilized due to its high strength, lightweight nature, and recyclability. PET resins are commonly found in plastic bottles, food containers, and synthetic fibers, making them integral to various industries.</p><p>
