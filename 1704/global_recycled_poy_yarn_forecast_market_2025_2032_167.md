<h1>Recycled POY Yarn Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Recycled POY (Partially Oriented Yarn) is a sustainable textile product created by reprocessing polyester waste from post-consumer or post-industrial sources. The process involves collecting polyester materials, such as plastic bottles or fabric scraps, and then re-spinning them into yarn through a specialized method. This yarn is referred to as "partially oriented" due to its partially stretched structure, which makes it suitable for further processing into various textile applications.</p><p>
</p><p>POY yarns are primarily used in the production of fabrics for apparel, industrial, and other uses, and they offer an environmentally friendly alternative to traditional polyester yarns, which are often derived from virgin fossil fuels.
