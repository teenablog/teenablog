</p><p>Anti-fog paint is a type of hydrophilic coating that contains polyvinyl alcohol. These coatings disperse small droplets of fog formed due to water condensation, making them non-mist coatings. They are transparent, abrasion-resistant, and durable, maintaining their quality even after extensive use.</p><p>
</p><p>Anti-fog paint refers to coatings that prevent the formation of fog on surfaces. They are commonly used in various applications like flat polycarbonate sheets, automotive anti-fog LED headlights, helmet goggles, and masks, and commercial freezer windows. These coatings have additional functionalities such as anti-fouling, waterproofing, and oil-proofing, meeting diverse application needs.</p><p>
