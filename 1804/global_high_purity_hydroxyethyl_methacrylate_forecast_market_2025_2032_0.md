</p><p>High Purity Hydroxyethyl Methacrylate (HEMA) is a colorless, transparent liquid primarily used as a special methacrylate monomer in coatings, reactive resins, and adhesives. Known for its excellent adhesion, flexibility, and water resistance, HEMA plays a critical role in various industrial applications, including medical devices, dental products, and polymer manufacturing. The high purity variant ensures minimal impurities, enhancing product performance and stability across diverse sectors.</p><p>
