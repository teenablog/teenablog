<h1>Sodium Tripolyphosphate (STPP) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Sodium Tripolyphosphate (STPP) is a white crystalline powder known for its superior mobility and solubility in water, forming an alkaline solution. It possesses a strong chelating ability with metal ions like calcium, magnesium, and iron, making it a crucial component in various industrial applications. STPP is extensively used as a detergent builder, food additive, and in other industrial sectors due to its ability to soften water, enhance cleaning efficiency, and preserve food quality.</p><p>
