<h1>Polyvinylpyrrolidone in Cosmetic Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Polyvinylpyrrolidone (PVP), also known as Polyvidone or Povidone, is a synthetic polymer derived from the polymerization of N-vinyl-3-pyrrolidone. It is widely used in the cosmetic industry due to its excellent film-forming, binding, and stabilizing properties. PVP enhances the texture and stability of cosmetic products, making it a key ingredient in sunscreens, makeup, and skincare formulations.</p><p>
