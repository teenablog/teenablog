<h1>Polyester Woven Dryer Fabrics Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Polyester woven dryer fabrics, also referred to as polyester dryer screens or conveyor belts, are specialized industrial fabrics primarily used in the papermaking process. These fabrics play a crucial role in drying paper pulp and paper sheets by facilitating moisture removal in the drying section of paper machines. Manufactured using high-quality polyester materials, these fabrics exhibit excellent thermal stability, durability, and resistance to wear and chemical exposure. They are designed in various structures, including single-layer and multi-layer configurations, to suit specific drying requirements.</p><p>
