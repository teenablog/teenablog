<h1>Process Chemicals for Water Treatment Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Process chemicals for water treatment refer to specialized chemical compounds used to enhance the efficiency and effectiveness of water purification processes. These chemicals aid in removing contaminants, neutralizing harmful substances, and ensuring the safe disposal or reuse of water. The primary categories of these chemicals include pH adjusters &amp; softeners, flocculants &amp; coagulants, corrosion inhibitors, scale inhibitors/dispersants, biocides &amp; disinfectants, among others. They play a crucial role in industrial, municipal, and commercial applications where water treatment is essential for maintaining regulatory compliance and environmental safety.</p><p>
