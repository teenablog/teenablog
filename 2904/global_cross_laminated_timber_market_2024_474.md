<h1>Cross Laminated Timber (CLT) Market, Global Outlook &amp; Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Cross Laminated Timber (CLT) is an engineered wood product gaining significant traction as a sustainable alternative to concrete and steel in the construction industry. CLT panels consist of multiple layers of solid wood boards arranged crosswise and bonded using adhesive or mechanical fasteners. This configuration enhances dimensional stability, structural strength, and load-bearing capacity, making it an ideal material for residential, commercial, and institutional buildings. The material is eco-friendly, offering reduced carbon emissions compared to traditional construction materials.</p><p>
