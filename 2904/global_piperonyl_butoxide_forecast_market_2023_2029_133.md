<h1>Piperonyl Butoxide (PBO) Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Piperonyl Butoxide (PBO) is a synthetic compound derived from benzodioxole. It is primarily used as an insecticide synergist, enhancing the effectiveness of active pesticide ingredients such as pyrethrins, pyrethroids, rotenone, and carbamate. By inhibiting the microsomal enzyme detoxification activity in insects, PBO ensures the prolonged efficacy of these pesticides. Its applications span home pest control, agriculture, and gardens, making it a vital component in integrated pest management solutions.</p><p>
