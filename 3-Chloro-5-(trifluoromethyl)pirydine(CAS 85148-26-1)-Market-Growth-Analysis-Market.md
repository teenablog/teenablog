3-Chloro-5-(trifluoromethyl)pyridine (CAS 85148-26-1) is a halogenated pyridine derivative with a molecular formula of C6H3ClF3N and a molecular weight of 181.54 g/mol. It is a colorless to slightly yellow liquid with a boiling point of 144°C and a density of 1.416 g/cm³. The compound is soluble in organic solvents and is used as a building block for the synthesis of various organic compounds, including pharmaceuticals, agrochemicals, and materials.
