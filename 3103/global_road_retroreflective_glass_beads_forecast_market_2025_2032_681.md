<h1>Road Retro-reflective Glass Beads Market, Global Outlook and Forecast 2025-2032</h1><p>Road retro-reflective glass beads are specialized materials used in road markings, traffic signs, and safety equipment to enhance visibility, particularly at night or in low-light conditions. These beads, embedded in traffic paints and thermoplastics, reflect light back toward the source, ensuring that lane markings and signs remain highly visible to drivers. Their application is critical for improving road safety and reducing accidents.</p><p>
