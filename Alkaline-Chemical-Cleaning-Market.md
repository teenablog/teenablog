Alkaline cleaning agents are chemicals used for cleaning that have a pH above 7.0. These agents are typically used to remove stubborn dirt, grime, and stains from surfaces. They are also effective at breaking down protein-based soils, such as blood and food residues. Alkaline cleaners are typically safe to use on most surfaces, but they can cause damage to some materials, so it is always best to test them on a small area first.
