key trends in the automotive diodes market:



Growing electrification of vehicles is driving demand as diodes play a crucial role in regulating electrical currents in ignition systems, battery charging circuits etc.
Manufacturers are developing high-power Schottky diodes and MOSFETs to efficiently handle increasing voltages in electric/hybrid vehicles.
Demand is shifting from low-power diodes to high-power diodes (>1A) that can operate at elevated temperatures without reliability issues.
Adoption of advanced driver-assistance systems, x-by-wire systems and infotainment is increasing usage of protection and switching diodes.
Customized diodes optimized for specific applications like DC-DC converters, on-board chargers are gaining traction.
Automakers focus on lightweighting - compact diodes with high current ratings are gaining importance to optimize space.
Asia Pacific dominates production due to presence of automotive manufacturing bases. However, onshoring efforts are ramping up capacities elsewhere.
Industry consolidation is ongoing as major component manufacturers acquire diode makers to strengthen automotive product portfolios.
Tightening environmental regulations increase the need for energy-efficient, recyclable diodes utilizing lead-free, RoHS compliant materials.
