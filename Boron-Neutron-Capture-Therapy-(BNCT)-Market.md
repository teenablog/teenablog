key trends in the Boron Neutron Capture Therapy (BNCT) market:



Advances in boron carriers for targeted delivery: New liposomes, nanoparticles, and compounds are being developed to enable precise boron delivery to cancer cells and improve treatment efficacy.

Combination with other therapies: Combining BNCT with chemotherapy, immunotherapy, or epigenetic modulation holds promise for synergistic effects against aggressive cancers.

Expanding indications tested beyond head & neck cancers: While historically used for brain tumors, trials for liver, lung, and metastatic cancers using BNCT are underway. Expanding indications can boost adoption.

Emergence of new centers & clinical trials globally: More hospitals and research centers, especially across Europe and Asia are acquiring capabilities and conducting trials, increasing access.

Refinement of neutron beam generation technologies: Efforts to develop more compact, lower cost accelerator and reactor-based neutron sources can make BNCT more accessible.
