Dipotassium Glycyrrhizinate is a natural ingredient derived from licorice root. It is a dipotassium salt of Glycyrrhizic Acid, which is a compound found in the roots of the licorice plant. Dipotassium Glycyrrhizinate has numerous benefits, especially in the field of skincare.
