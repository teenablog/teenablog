Key Trends:



Tech-driven services - FM service providers are increasingly leveraging IoT sensors, building information modelling, automation, AR/VR and other technologies to enable data-led smart facilities and enhanced monitoring.

Sustainability focus - Demand for FM services supporting net zero emissions, energy efficiency, water conservation goals and overall sustainable operations is rising across client organisations.

Integrated service models - Shift towards consolidated, integrated facilities management rather than a piecemeal approach for consistency across property and workplace portfolios.

Specialization - Facility managers developing distinct expertise around critical infrastructures like data centers, healthcare facilities, industrial sites etc. catering to industry specific needs.

Advanced analytics - Intelligent analysis around utilization patterns, breakdown probability, energy optimization, return-to-work planning using AI and machine learning techniques.

Emergency preparedness - Improved risk management, business continuity planning and crisis response mechanisms being built into FM delivery models.
