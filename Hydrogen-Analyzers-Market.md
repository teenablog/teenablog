Uses of Hydrogen Analyzers



Hydrogen analyzers are used in a variety of industries and applications. They can be used to measure the concentration of hydrogen in gases, liquids, and solids. Hydrogen analyzers are used in research and development, quality control, and industrial process applications.



Hydrogen analyzers can be used to measure the purity of hydrogen gas produced by electrolysis or other methods. Hydrogen gas is often used as a fuel or reductant in chemical processes. Purity levels of 99.9999% are required for many applications.



High-purity hydrogen is also used as an inert gas shield for arc welding and other thermal processes where oxygen could cause contamination.

In research laboratories, hydrogen analyzers are used to monitor reactions involving hydrogen gas or solutions containing hydrogen ions (acids).



They can also be used to determine the equilibrium concentrations of dissolved hydrogen ions in solution. Quality control personnel use hydrogen analyzers to monitor the composition of gases produced during manufacturing processes.
