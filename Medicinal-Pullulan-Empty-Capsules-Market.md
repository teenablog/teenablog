Market Overview

Medicinal Pullulan Empty Capsules are capsules made from pullulan, a natural polysaccharide derived through microbial fermentation of plant-based raw materials. These capsules are an ideal solution for health-conscious consumers and those with dietary restrictions, as they are derived from plant sources and are suitable for vegetarians, vegans, and individuals adhering to specific religious diets such as Islamic and Jewish cultures.

The raw material used in the production of pullulan is obtained through a fermentation process using starch from plants like corn, wheat, and rice, making it free from animal-derived components. This is one of the key reasons for the growing preference for pullulan-based capsules over traditional gelatin capsules. Medicinal Pullulan Empty Capsules are often used in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries to encapsulate medications, dietary supplements, and functional foods.
