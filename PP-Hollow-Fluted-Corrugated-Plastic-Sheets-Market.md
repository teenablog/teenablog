Polypropylene twinwall sheet, commonly known as fluted polypropylene or corrugated plastic, is a lightweight yet durable material widely used across various industries. This polyolefin-based product features a unique fluted design, providing enhanced strength while maintaining flexibility.

The material is popular for applications such as packaging, signage, construction, and automotive industries due to its affordability, water resistance, and ease of customization.
