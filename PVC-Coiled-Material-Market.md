The global PVC Coiled Material market was valued at USD 79 million in 2023 and is projected to reach USD 114.16 million by 2032, growing at a CAGR of 5.40% during the forecast period.

PVC Coiled Material is a high-performance polymer waterproofing sheet composed of PVC resin, plasticizers, stabilizers, and anti-aging agents. It is manufactured using an extrusion process, ensuring high tensile strength, excellent flexibility, low-temperature resistance, and superior aging resistance. The material is widely used across various industries, including construction, automotive, and industrial applications, due to its durability and water resistance.
