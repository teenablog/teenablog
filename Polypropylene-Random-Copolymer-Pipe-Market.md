Market Size

Polypropylene Random Copolymer Pipe (PPR-C) is a type of thermoplastic piping material made from Polypropylene Random Copolymer Type 3. It is widely recognized for its high durability, chemical resistance, and ability to transport potable hot and cold water efficiently. PPR pipes conform to DIN8078 standards, ensuring their suitability for use in plumbing, heating, and industrial applications. Their long service life, low thermal conductivity, and corrosion resistance make them an ideal choice for water distribution systems in residential, commercial, and industrial settings.
