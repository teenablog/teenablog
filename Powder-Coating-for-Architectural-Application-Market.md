Market Size

Powder coating for architectural applications refers to the process of applying a dry powder to materials such as aluminum, steel, and other metals used in construction. This coating provides both aesthetic and protective benefits, enhancing durability, corrosion resistance, and weather protection. It is widely used in commercial, industrial, and residential buildings, offering an eco-friendly alternative to traditional liquid paints.
