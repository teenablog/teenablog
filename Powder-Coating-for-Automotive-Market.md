Market Overview

Powder coating for automotive applications is a dry finishing process widely used to enhance the durability, corrosion resistance, and aesthetics of automotive parts and components. Unlike traditional liquid paint, powder coatings use electrostatically charged particles that adhere to metal or plastic surfaces before being cured under heat. This results in a robust, even, and environmentally friendly coating.

Powder coatings are commonly applied to wheels, chassis, engine parts, and body panels, offering superior protection against harsh environmental conditions, UV radiation, and mechanical wear.
