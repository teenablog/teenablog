Market Overview

Powder coating for indoor applications refers to the use of dry powder-based coatings formulated specifically for interior surfaces and products. Unlike traditional liquid coatings, powder coatings provide a durable, environmentally friendly, and cost-effective finishing solution. These coatings are commonly used on household appliances, furniture, indoor metal fixtures, and automotive components. The growing demand for sustainable and high-performance coatings has driven the market’s expansion, particularly in industrial and commercial indoor applications.
