Semiconductor Etch Equipment Market
Semiconductor etch equipment is used to remove material from the surface of a semiconductor wafer. The process is an important part of semiconductor manufacturing and can be used to create features with very precise dimensions. Etching is a process in which layers from the surface of a wafer are removed using chemicals. Semiconductor etch equipment is used copiously in various semiconductor fabrication processes.



Global Semiconductor Etch Equipment Market is expected to reach USD 41.73 Billion by 2028 in terms of revenue. In 2021, the market was valued USD 19.61 Billion in terms of revenue the CAGR is 14.13%. In terms of volume, semiconductor etch equipment market size was 20,328 Units in 2021 and expected to reach 47,424 units by 2028.
