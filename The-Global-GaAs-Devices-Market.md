Factors Driving Growth in GaAs Devices Market:
Increasing demand for GaAs devices across various industries: GaAs devices are witnessing a rise in demand from industries like aerospace and defense, electronics, and communications. This can be attributed to the growing adoption of GaAs devices within these sectors.
Escalating need for high-speed data transmission: The demand for high-speed data transmission is on the rise, which is directly fueling the demand for GaAs devices. These devices are extensively used in applications involving high-speed data transmission, such as 5G networks and fiber optic networks.
Government initiatives promoting GaAs device usage: Governments worldwide are actively encouraging the use of GaAs devices. This initiative aims to reduce dependence on imported semiconductors and enhance the security of the supply chain.
Challenges in GaAs Devices Market:
High cost of GaAs devices: The market growth is hindered by the relatively high cost associated with GaAs devices. This cost factor poses a restraint on the overall expansion of the market.
Competition from alternative semiconductor materials: GaAs devices face tough competition from alternative semiconductor materials like silicon and indium phosphide. These materials are less expensive than GaAs and are available in larger quantities, thereby posing a competitive challenge to GaAs devices.
Recent developments in the GaAs devices market:
In February 2023, Qorvo announced the expansion of its GaAs foundry capacity in Japan. The expansion will allow Qorvo to meet the growing demand for GaAs devices in the automotive, industrial, and defense markets.
In March 2023, Infineon Technologies announced the development of a new GaAs power amplifier for 5G networks. The amplifier is designed to meet the power and performance requirements of 5G networks.
In April 2023, Broadcom acquired 5G semiconductor company RaDaRIQ. The acquisition will allow Broadcom to expand its portfolio of GaAs devices for 5G networks.
