Wafer Chucks include two type, electrostatic chuck and Vacuum Wafer Chucks. The vacuum chucks are used in the semiconductor industry to accurately position the wafer in the machine. Vacuum Wafer Chuck is mainly used for semiconductor dicing, grinding and washing etc.

The Vacuum Wafer Chucks Market is a sector that involves the manufacturing and sale of vacuum wafer chucks. These chucks are devices that are used in the semiconductor industry for securely holding and positioning silicon wafers during various manufacturing processes like deposition, etching, and inspection.
